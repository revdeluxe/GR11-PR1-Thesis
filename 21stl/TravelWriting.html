Caayunan

One of the rural areas around Basud, Bicol
Although the economy there is not for tourism, I will still show you
around.

Economy

There's nothing special about caayunan, rather it's not trying to be
somebody else. the life is mediocre nothing more nothing less..

Climate

Since Caayunan is located on the far eastern side of the philippines multiple
storm surges which may sound quite scary. the climate is not cold nor hot
it's basically the kind of province you think of when making trips.
And yet, nothing more is greater than a morning dew.

Attraction

Aside from food locals made quite impressive resorts and cottages around rivers
which I tell you, It's great if you want to forget your worries and reward yourself.
